## 🔴 CRITICAL: McGill University Exposé on AI Health Videos

**Source:** McGill University Office for Science and Society (Dec 11, 2025)
**Author:** Jonathan Jarry, M.Sc.

This is the most important piece of intelligence for our channel. McGill published a detailed investigation into AI-generated health channels targeting seniors. Key findings:

### What They Found:
- **"Senior Secrets"** channel: 321K subscribers, 17M+ total views, posts every few days
- Titles like: "Goodbye old age!", "Never take this after 65!", "Just 1 cup before bed repairs your eyes overnight"
- **Fake doctors** with no verifiable credentials (e.g., "Dr. Thomas Durand," "Dr. Camille Dubois")
- **Fabricated studies:** Investigation of 65 scientific references across 4 channels found only **5 were real**; 60 were hallucinated
- **Content farms in Vietnam** traced via metadata (Vietnamese geotags, French channels tagged "Pháp" = Vietnam)
- Revenue model: YouTube ads only (no products sold)

### Why This Matters for Dr. Kane:
1. **This is exactly what YouTube is cracking down on.** These channels are the PRIMARY target of the 2026 "inauthentic content" enforcement.
2. **We must be the OPPOSITE of these channels.** Real credentials, real studies, real expertise, transparent AI use.
3. **Our "Doctor Who Explains WHY" positioning is validated.** These fake channels don't explain mechanisms — they just make sensational claims. We do the opposite.
4. **Citing real studies with proper journal names is a competitive advantage.** 60 out of 65 references were fake on these channels. Ours will be real.

### Detection Tips from McGill (What Viewers Are Learning):
- Incorrect anatomy in visuals
- Unnatural-looking people (too smooth, odd lighting)
- Monotonous or slightly off voiceover
- Gibberish text on screen (fake journal names)
- **Next-gen AI (Sora, Nano Banana Pro) is making detection harder**

### Our Defense:
✅ Use YouTube's "Altered Content" toggle
✅ Cite real studies with verifiable journal names
✅ Dr. Kane's warm, conversational style (not monotonous)
✅ Explain mechanisms (not just sensational claims)
✅ Original commentary and opinion in every video
✅ Quality over quantity (3-4 uploads/week, not daily)
