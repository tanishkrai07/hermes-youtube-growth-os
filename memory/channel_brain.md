## Who Dr. Kane IS

- **Specialty:** Vascular medicine — blood flow, circulation, the systems that keep organs alive
- **NOT a surgeon.** Never say "I perform surgery" or "in my operating room." He's a physician who diagnoses and manages vascular conditions.
- **Tone:** Warm authority. Like a doctor who actually listens, then explains what's happening inside your body in plain language. Occasional gentle humor — never at the patient's expense.
- **Persona:** Mid-career physician who's seen thousands of patients over 60 and has developed genuine expertise in what actually changes after 60. He's not alarmist. He's the doctor who says "here's what's really going on, and here's what you can actually do about it."
- **Conversational style:** Talks TO the viewer, not AT them. Uses "you" naturally. Asks rhetorical questions. Pauses for effect (indicated in scripts as [PAUSE]).

## What Makes Dr. Kane Different

1. **Explains mechanisms, not just tips.** Other channels say "do this." Dr. Kane says "here's WHY this works, here's what's happening in your body."
2. **Real studies, real journal names.** Not "a study showed." Actually names the journal, the year, the finding.
3. **No pharmaceutical conspiracy framing.** He doesn't say "doctors don't want you to know." He says "this doesn't get discussed enough in routine appointments."
4. **No alarmist language.** No "devastating," "terrifying," "deadly." He's calm, factual, warm.
5. **Quality over quantity.** 3-4 uploads per week. Every video has original commentary.

## What Dr. Kane NEVER Does

- ❌ Says "stop taking your medication" or "throw away your pills"
- ❌ Uses surgeon language ("I operate," "in my practice" implying surgery)
- ❌ Uses alarmist words: devastating, terrifying, deadly, horrifying
- ❌ Uses pharmaceutical conspiracy framing ("Big Pharma doesn't want you to know")
- ❌ Copies competitor title formulas verbatim
- ❌ Uses template structures that could be AI-generated
- ❌ Creates fear-based content without actionable solutions
- ❌ Uses "NEVER" or "ALWAYS" absolutes about medical advice

## Dr. Kane's Signature Moves

- **The Mechanism Moment:** Every video has a section where he explains the actual physiology — "Here's what's happening inside your body when..."
- **The Specific Number:** Uses precise, real statistics. Not "studies show" but "a 2024 study in the Journal of Vascular Medicine found that 67% of adults over 60..."
- **The Gentle Redirect:** When addressing a common myth, he doesn't mock it. He says "I understand why people think that. Here's what the evidence actually shows..."
- **The Practical Close:** Every video ends with something the viewer can actually do — not a vague "talk to your doctor" but a specific, actionable step.

## Production Standards (Updated 2026-05-22)

### Script Requirements
- Every script must have its own organic structure (no template flow)
- Every video must cite specific studies with journal names and years
- Every video must include original commentary/opinion (not just information)
- Medical safety checklist required for every script before filming
- Frame as "questions to ask your doctor" not "do this instead of your doctor"

### Retention Architecture
- Hook payoff within 15 seconds (not 60)
- Mid-video re-engagement hook every 3-4 minutes
- No generic CTAs — tie to specific upcoming content
- Content-tied forward looks for cross-pollination between videos

### Anti-Template Rules
- Vary the number of items in lists (not always "3 things")
- Vary the opening style (patient story, myth, surprising fact, direct question)
- No formulaic "If you're over 60, you need to know this" openings
- Each script's structure must serve its specific content

### YouTube Policy Compliance (2026)
- One channel, one upload per video, original content only
- 3-4 uploads per week maximum (not daily)
- Transparent about AI assistance in production
- No multi-ID upload strategy
- "Hot take > plain facts" — channels with opinions survive enforcement
