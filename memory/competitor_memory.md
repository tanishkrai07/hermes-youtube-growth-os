# Competitor Memory

Last updated: 2026-05-16

## Known Competitor Data

The initial dataset contains:

- Competitor transcript text from high-performing elder-health videos.
- SOP references to 109 competitor videos.
- Examples from Dr. Michael Kent, Dr. Adewale, Dr. Waterling, Dr. James Hargrove, Doctor Leana, Doctor Becker, and others.

## Current High-Performance Patterns

- Warning and NEVER frames perform strongly.
- Specific time hooks perform best: 30-second, 90-second, 2-minute, 3-minute.
- Medication danger, reheated food danger, night peeing, blood pressure, and memory fear are proven demand areas.
- Food/drink revelations work best when they focus on one familiar item and one shocking result.
- Open-lane opportunity: leg circulation and muscle loss have high average views but fewer competitor videos.
