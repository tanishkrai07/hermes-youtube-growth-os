### Morning Protocol (9:00-11:30)
**The 60-Second Morning Stem Cell Boost:**

1. **Preparation (Night Before)**:
   - Grind 2 tablespoons of organic flaxseed in a coffee grinder
   - Store in airtight container in refrigerator (prevents oxidation)
   - *Do not pre-grind more than 3 days worth - the oils go rancid*

2. **Morning Consumption**:
   - Mix 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed with 4 oz warm water
   - Add squeeze of lemon (vitamin C enhances lignan absorption)
   - Optional: sprinkle of cinnamon (blood sugar regulation)
   - Drink immediately on empty stomach, wait 20 minutes before breakfast

3. **Optimization Tips**:
   - Best taken with vitamin C-rich foods (citrus, bell peppers)
   - Avoid taking with calcium supplements (can bind to fiber)
   - Consistent daily use shows better results than sporadic
   - Monitor effects: improved digestion, joint comfort, energy levels within 2-4 weeks
