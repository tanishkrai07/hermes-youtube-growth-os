# Idea Generation Prompt

Read the current context pack, idea bank, competitor memory, and winning patterns.

Generate 10 video ideas for the requested pillar or week.

For each idea, include:

- Working title under 70 characters with Dr. Victor Kane and age marker.
- Pillar
- Persona A or B
- Hook type
- List spine with 5-9 items
- Hope close
- Score out of 20 with dimension breakdown
- Why now
- What competitor signal supports it

Rules:

- Never show ideas below 15/20.
- Prefer underserved high-value pillars when tied.
- Every idea must have an immediate free or low-cost solution.
